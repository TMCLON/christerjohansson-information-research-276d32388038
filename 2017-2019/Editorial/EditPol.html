Editorial Policy

Information Research is a free, international, scholarly journal, dedicated to making freely accessible the results of research across a wide range of information-related disciplines. A glance at the subject index will show the scope. We publish both refereed papers and working papers in the fields of information science, information management, information systems, information policy and librarianship.

Papers may be of any length, and should be submitted in HTML format according to the Instructions for Authors (word processors such as Word provide for saving in HTML, but if an author experiences problems, he or she should contact the Editor). Each paper should be submitted with an abstract and with the figures as separate .gif or .jpg files.

Information Research also publishes book reviews and software reviews and has a page devoted to electronic journals and newsletters in fields within the scope of the journal.

Information Research is published and maintained by Professor Tom Wilson. t.d.wilson@sheffield.ac.uk Last up-date: 21st March 1999.
