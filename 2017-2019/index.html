Information Research, is an open access, international, peer-reviewed, scholarly journal, dedicated to making accessible the results of research across a wide range of information-related disciplines. It is published by the University of Borås, Sweden, with financial support from the Nordic Board for Periodicals in the Humanities and Social Sciences, and edited by Professor T.D. Wilson. It is hosted, and given technical support, by Lund University Libraries, Sweden.
