Active collaboration between archives, libraries and museums started earlier than the political agenda was formulated at the European level. The network applied for funding for a doctoral school several times, and finally received a grant for four years (2011-2015). Presently, the network has twelve institutional members: six departments or schools from five universities, and six memory institutions.
